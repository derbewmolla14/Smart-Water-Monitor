About CAWST

CAWST (Centre for Affordable Water and Sanitation Technology) is a Canadian charity that provides training and consulting to help people access safe water and sanitation. HydroOS uses CAWST standards for water treatment monitoring.

Welcome to HydroOS

HydroOS is an innovative Smart Water Monitoring platform designed to bridge the gap between Water Engineering and Software Technology.

Developed to ensure clean water access and efficient resource management, our system provides real-time data tracking for university communities.

  • Real-time Water Quality Monitoring
  • Automated Alert Systems
  • Secure Payment Tracking
  • Smart Resource Dashboard

"Engineering a sustainable future through smart digital solutions."
